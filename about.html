About CoderDojo Banbridge

Empowering young minds through code and creativity

Our Story

CoderDojo Banbridge is a free, volunteer-led coding club where young people aged 7-17 learn to code, develop websites, apps, programs, and games. We're part of the Code Club community, which is run by the Raspberry Pi Foundation and connects millions of young people worldwide to the power of computing and digital technologies.

// Welcome to the world of code!
print("Hello, future coders!")

Our volunteer mentors are passionate about technology and love sharing their knowledge. Whether you're taking your first steps in coding or already building amazing projects, we provide a supportive environment where creativity and learning flourish together.

What We Do

  • Learn programming languages: Scratch, Python, HTML/CSS, JavaScript, and more
  • Build amazing projects: websites, games, mobile apps, and even robots
  • Collaborate and share: work with peers and showcase your creations
  • Problem-solving: develop computational thinking and logical reasoning
  • Digital citizenship: learn to use technology safely and responsibly
  • Real-world skills: prepare for future careers in technology
Free • Fun • Inclusive • Inspiring

Part of Something Amazing

When you join CoderDojo Banbridge, you become part of the global Code Club community - a network that has reached over 2 million young people across 100+ countries. Research shows that 90% of young people who participate in Code Club activities increase their skills and independence in coding.
