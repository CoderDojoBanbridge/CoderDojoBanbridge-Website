About CoderDojo

CoderDojo Banbridge is a free, volunteer-led coding club where young people aged 7-17 learn to code, develop websites, apps, programs, and games. We're part of the global CoderDojo Foundation network that helps kids around the world discover the magic of programming!

// Welcome to the world of code!
print("Hello, CoderDojo!")

Our mentors are passionate volunteers who love sharing their knowledge and helping young coders grow. No prior experience needed - just bring your curiosity and imagination!

What We Do

  • Learn programming languages like Scratch, Python, HTML/CSS, and JavaScript
  • Build amazing projects: websites, games, apps, and robots
  • Collaborate with fellow young coders and mentors
  • Participate in coding challenges and hackathons
  • Share your creations with the community
  • Connect with CoderDojos around the world
Free • Fun • For Everyone

Part of Something Bigger

CoderDojo is a global movement with over 2,000 clubs in 100+ countries! When you join CoderDojo Banbridge, you're becoming part of this amazing worldwide community of young coders, mentors, and innovators.
